Loving Jesus,
Sharing his Love




Here at Campbell Street, we believe that young people are an integral part in our faith community. Everything that we do is purposed for our students to get more connected to the church community, their families, and Christ.



While youth ministry is an important avenue for spiritual growth in young people, it isn't everything. We believe that our ministry should be used as a supplement to the faith development that is happening at home through the family.
