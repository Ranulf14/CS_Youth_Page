Our events are designed to help the teens grow and connect with God and with each other. The events are meant to be fun and enjoyable, but also to be challenging spiritually.


Passion Senior Retreat: Passion is a massive youth conference in Atlanta Georgia. "Passion exists to glorify God by uniting students in worship, prayer and justice for spiritual awakening in this generation" (from Passion's webpage). This is a senior retreat, because Passion is only open to those age 18-25 (January 1-3)


Winterfest:Winterfest is a youth convention for teens across the southwest. It is powerful opportunity to praise and worship with others of the same age. It takes place in the city of Gatlinburg, and the time there usually includes some free time in the city. (February 16-18)
